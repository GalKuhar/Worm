Crossover: Bolo
Rating: Not Rated
Summary:

Some time ago when I started thinking about a Worm fic, I had three ideas that came to mind. One turned into Taylor Varga, which grew rather out of hand, although I regret nothing! One I did about eight chapters of, but shelved for the time being as it was rapidly turning into something ridiculously complicated. I may come back to it at some point in the future even so. I've lifted a few bits out of it for Taylor Varga, so it wasn't a waste of time.

The third idea was the one behind this beginning. Whether I'll go on with it at some point I don't know. I quite like the idea, but I haven't decided exactly how it would work, aside from it being an AU of Worm that breaks entirely with continuity some years before the start of canon. No Scion, that golden idiot makes the entire setting almost impossible to end properly without nearly everyone dying or a huge deus ex machina, so he snuffed it at the same time as Eden. Less powerful Endbringers, for the same reason, although still insanely powerful. Basically, tilting the field a little more towards sanity.

It would have been fairly dark even so, although not Worm dark. I didn't want to mow down entire worlds full of people...

The host thread writes the trigger event.

After the character is created in the shard thread, the host thread will create a trigger event. Configure is a shard specialized in creating, tweaking, and adjusting powers, and more often than not will include those abilities in the powers it grants its host. But a strong foundation must still be made before any shenanigans can be built on top, and the trigger event will affect how the power is applied and expressed. It's impossible to precisely determine the outcome of a trigger event (translation: don't try to design the power itself), but clearly defined trigger events often have predictable tendencies. For instance, a trigger defined by social isolation will always result in powers with a strong Master component, and so on. You can even go for a multitrigger if you like. Just keep in mind that, no, you can't have the clusterfuck trigger mindmeld a la Rain O'Fire, and also your cluster mates aren't controllable characters. Second triggers are not something you can actively decide on, although a sufficiently severe trigger event might result in a second trigger.

The host must be an unpowered person in the city of Brockton Bay, in Earth Bet, at a time no later than the date Worm starts on. Original characters are allowed, within reason. Entire families can be invented wholesale, and even entire streets, as long as they conform to the vaguely defined geography of Brockton Bay and don't contradict anything that already exists. OC parahumans, on the other hand, aren't allowed; you can use existing parahumans in the unpowered host's backstory, as long as it's reasonable and doesn't contradict canon. The host can belong to whatever organizations they could reasonably access given their location, disposition, resources, age, and so on. Feel free to include details such as lifestyle, personality, hobbies, friendships, career, education, and whatnot. Anything that's important to the character will, more likely than not, have at least a passing importance to the story.
