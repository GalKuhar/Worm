Summary:
The host thread writes the trigger event.

After the character is created in the shard thread, the host thread will create a trigger event. Configure is a shard specialized in creating, tweaking, and adjusting powers, and more often than not will include those abilities in the powers it grants its host. But a strong foundation must still be made before any shenanigans can be built on top, and the trigger event will affect how the power is applied and expressed. It's impossible to precisely determine the outcome of a trigger event (translation: don't try to design the power itself), but clearly defined trigger events often have predictable tendencies. For instance, a trigger defined by social isolation will always result in powers with a strong Master component, and so on. You can even go for a multitrigger if you like. Just keep in mind that, no, you can't have the clusterfuck trigger mindmeld a la Rain O'Fire, and also your cluster mates aren't controllable characters. Aiming for multiple power categories is also a possibility. Second triggers are not something you can actively decide on, although a sufficiently severe trigger event might result in a second trigger.
